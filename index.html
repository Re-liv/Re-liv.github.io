About us

Senior citizens have limited choices to educate and entertain themselves to overcome the loneliness and boredom, so, we wanted to do something to help them maintain their emotional as well as physical well-being regardless of geographies and socio-economic backgrounds. We at Relive have undertaken this initiative of helping our senior citizens find a sense of purpose and renewed confidence through our engaging and understanding.

Vision

Keeping the emotional well-being of the elderly in mind, Re.Liv is conceptualized as one-of-a-kind offering has been created to help seniors find a renewed sense of belonging by attending a host of exciting events and forming new meaningful connections.

Mission

Consistently trying to uphold the positive spirits of Senior population by strengthening the well being and healthy ecosystem.
